Persona study

Deutsche Post

About

The ongoing persona study presents different user types that visits a post office and use specific post services there.
The data obtained visualizes their needs for their visit and shows the potential of each persona group to use Deutsche Post’s services via digital solutions instead of visiting their local post office.

Concept

The persona project started with several focus group sessions to identify potential user groups of post office customers. Afterwards an online survey enriched and refined the user groups.

my tasks

Illustration, data visualisation.

tools

Sketch and Adobe Illustrator for graphic design,
InDesign and PowerPoint for Layouting.

Why Powerpoint?

At an early stage I started with the layouting tool Adobe Indesign to define the look of the report pages. Afterwards I translated the final design into a PowerPoint file to make sure other non-designer colleagues are able to edit the data.

The style

The simple and reduced shapes simplify the characters and enable to reuse for additional figures. The textures used for coloring and shading gives the artwork a handmade, more organic look.
