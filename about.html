About

My Story

After studying game design at MD.H, Munich, I started at Deutsche Post, Bonn, as a game and graphics designer for their former website buzzerdeal.de. I enjoyed concepting, pitching and prototyping various mini games for customers and stakeholders.

After my role as a game designer, I took the oppurtunity to expand my professional experience by starting as a Junior UX Designer in Berlin. There, at Deutsche Post’s UX department I was able to transfer my knowledge in level designing for a mostly fun-loving audience into a broader mindset of user-centered design and all-embracing user journeys.

My tasks are mainly concepting, wireframing, designing and prototyping the user experiences or applications. I love to start new projects with Google’s Design Sprint method and already got the oppurtunity to facilitate two of these kind of sprints. I am looking forward to enhance my presentation and communication skills with each additional Design Sprint.

The close cooperation with my research and analytics collegues makes it easy to iterate new concepts based on user insights or to plan the fidelity of a prototype for a user test. I am on training for HTML/CSS and was already able to provide code snippets for forma!ing cookie notes or for A/B Tests via Adobe Target. By this and by a!ending learning courses I want to develop into a frontend designer and being able to implement my own designs via HTML, CSS and Javascript.
